## Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1. Visit [rigeo.com](https://rigeo.com)
2. Click on "Log in" in the top right corner
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Check your email for a verification link and click it
5. You'll be redirected to create your profile

### Logging In
1. Visit [rigeo.com](https://rigeo.com)
2. Click on "Log in" in the top right corner
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click "Sign In"

## Creating Your Profile

Your profile is your digital business card on REGEO. Make it stand out!

### Profile Basics
- **Display Name**: How others will see you (e.g., "Alex Chen")
- **Username**: Your unique handle (e.g., "@alexchen")
- **Bio**: A short description of who you are and what you do (max 160 characters)
- **Personal Quote**: A motto or favorite quote that represents you

### Avatar
Choose from several avatar styles or use your own image:
1. Select an avatar style that matches your personality
2. Your avatar will appear on your profile and throughout the platform

### Social Links
Connect your other online profiles:
- GitHub
- LinkedIn
- Twitter/X
- Personal website
- Other relevant links

## Showcasing Projects

Projects are the heart of REGEO. Show off your work and get discovered!

### Adding a Project
1. Go to your profile page
2. Click "Add Project"
3. Fill in the project details:
   - **Title**: The name of your project
   - **Description**: What your project does and why it's awesome
   - **Banner URL**: A visual representation of your project (optional)
   - **Project Link**: A link to your project (website, GitHub repo, etc.)

### Managing Projects
- **Edit**: Update project details at any time
- **Delete**: Remove projects you no longer want to showcase
- **View Stats**: See how many views and upvotes your projects receive

## Engaging with the Community

### Upvoting

### Exploring
Discover new creators through:

### Following
Stay updated with your favorite creators:
1. Visit a profile
2. Click the "Follow" button
3. Their updates will appear in your feed (coming soon)

## Understanding the Leaderboard

The leaderboard is where creators compete to be seen!

### How Scoring Works
Your score is calculated based on:
- **Upvotes**: Each upvote = 40 points
- **Profile Views**: Each view = 30 points
- **Streak**: Each day you visit = 20 points
- **Projects**: Each project = 10 points

### Leaderboard Sections
- **Today**: Rankings based on today's activity
- **Yesterday**: Rankings from yesterday
- **All-Time**: Cumulative rankings
- **Newcomers**: Highlighting new creators

### Climbing the Ranks
1. Encourage others to view your profile
2. Add quality projects
3. Visit REGEO daily to maintain your streak
4. Engage with the community by upvoting others

## Tracking Your Progress

Monitor your growth through the analytics dashboard.

### Accessing Analytics
1. Click "Dashboard" in the navigation menu
2. View your performance metrics

### Key Metrics
- **Views**: How many people have seen your profile
- **Upvotes**: How many people have shown appreciation
- **Rank**: Your position on the leaderboard
- **Streak**: How many consecutive days you've visited
- **Project Performance**: Stats for each of your projects

### Performance Insights
- **Best Performing Day**: When you got the most engagement
- **Peak Hour**: When your audience is most active
- **Engagement Rate**: How often viewers upvote your profile
- **Visitor Retention**: How many visitors return

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't Log In**
- Make sure you're using the correct email and password
- Check your spam folder for the verification email
- Try resetting your password

**Profile Not Updating**
- Refresh the page to see changes
- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser

**Projects Not Showing**
- Make sure all required fields are filled
- Check that your project link is valid
- Refresh the page

**Leaderboard Not Updating**
- Leaderboard updates may take a few minutes
- Try refreshing the page
- If the issue persists, contact support

### Browser Requirements

- Chrome (latest version)
- Firefox (latest version)
- Safari (latest version)
- Edge (latest version)

### Mobile Experience
REGEO is fully responsive and works on mobile devices. For the best experience:
- Use the latest version of your mobile browser
